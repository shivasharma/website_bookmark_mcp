MCP Setup Wizard

This page helps you connect Bookmark MCP to Claude Desktop, Cursor, or VS Code in 2 minutes.

Before you start: login first at /register, then come back here.

Step 1: Generate your secure token

Click once. This token links MCP to your account only.

Step 2: Copy config for your app

Pick your app, copy, then paste into MCP settings.

Step 3: Restart your app

Done. Restart Claude/Cursor/VS Code and your Bookmark tools will appear.
